# Garden-Layout-Helper
🌱 Garden Layout Helper
Stop guessing where things go. Plan your garden on a grid before you ever touch a shovel.

What It Does
Garden Layout Helper gives you a simple, visual grid to map out your garden beds — drag and drop plants, customize your space, and see exactly what fits where before you commit to anything in the real world.

Paint your beds — click a plant, then click cells to place it. Change your mind? Right-click to erase.
Customize everything — adjust the grid to match your actual garden dimensions, pick your own plant colors, and add any plant that isn't already in the list.
Save your work — not feeling a layout? Save it to one of three slots and experiment freely. Come back to any saved design whenever you want.
Built-in presets — jumpstart your planning with ready-made layouts like Cottage Garden, Three Sisters, Salad Garden, or Pollinator's Corner, all based on real companion planting logic.
Plant tracker — a live count shows exactly how many of each plant you've placed.


Why Bother?
Because digging up a tomato you planted in the wrong spot is nobody's idea of a good time. A few minutes planning on a grid saves hours of frustration in the dirt.

How to Use It

Pick a plant from the palette on the right
Click (or click and drag) to paint it onto the grid
Adjust rows and columns to match your actual space
Save layouts you like, keep experimenting with the rest
When the grid looks right — go dig
